# Alpha Civil Service Pension Calculator

## Overview

This project is a **pension calculator for the Alpha Civil Service pension scheme**. It allows users to estimate their pension based on salary progression, contributions, and additional pension purchases over time.

The tool is designed to provide a simple and flexible way to model pension outcomes, including early retirement scenarios and varying retirement ages.

---

## Features

* Calculate pension based on **annual salary**
* Support for **added pension contributions** (monthly or yearly)
* Configurable **retirement age**
* Ability to model **early retirement impacts**
* Clear breakdown of projected pension values over time

---

## How It Works

The calculator uses:

* Your **salary inputs**
* Your **contribution assumptions**
* Any **added pension contributions**
* Your **target retirement age**

It then projects your pension value based on Alpha scheme principles and provides an estimate of your expected pension at retirement.
